# Verdant Product Context

## Identity

Verdant is a **standalone grow room operating system**. It is its own product, its own brand, and its own company.
Verdant is **not** Next Door Cannabis. Do not associate the two in code, copy, fixtures, or docs.

## Mission

Help home and serious hobby cultivators turn diary entries, photos, sensor snapshots, and cautious AI analysis into safer grow decisions and better harvest outcomes — without taking control of their equipment.

## Build order (priority)

1. **Diary first** — Plant memory is the foundation. Quick Log, Timeline, photos.
2. **Sensors second** — Manual snapshots, then verified ingestion. Always labeled.
3. **AI third** — Cautious, context-aware advisor. Suggests only.
4. **Automation last** — Approval-required actions. No blind autopilot.

## Non-negotiable principles

- **No fake live data.** Demo data is always labeled demo.
- **No blind automation.** AI suggests, the grower approves.
- **No device control.** Verdant does not execute equipment changes.
- **Approval-required actions.** Every Action Queue item is grower-gated.
- **Hardware-neutral.** Verdant integrates with sensor sources (Raspberry Pi, Home Assistant, MQTT, CSV, manual). It does not depend on any single vendor and does not reverse-engineer closed hardware.
- **Read-only, no-write, no-control architecture** for AI and alerting surfaces.

## What Verdant is not

- Not a notebook.
- Not an irrigation controller.
- Not a closed-loop autopilot.
- Not a partner-hardware diagnostic tool.
- Not Next Door Cannabis.
