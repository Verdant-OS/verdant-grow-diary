# Verdant — 30-Day Grower-Intent SEO Content Plan

## A. Keyword strategy summary

- **Do not chase ERP/compliance head terms first.** Semrush US data shows
  the visible volume around "cannabis cultivation software" (~70/mo) is
  small, and the larger adjacent volume clusters (Metrc, Distru, Trym,
  Canix, GrowFlow, cannabis software) are dominated by licensed-operator
  seed-to-sale ERPs, dispensary POS tools, and state compliance
  trackers. That is **not Verdant's ICP**.
- **Target grower-problem long tails.** Serious home growers and
  sensor-driven DIY / small craft cultivators search for concrete
  problems ("grow diary app", "grow room VPD tracker", "AC Infinity
  data logging", "AI grow doctor"), not category ERPs.
- **Map pages/posts to real product value.** Each post should tie back
  to a Verdant value driver (Plant memory, Sensor truth, Gear you
  already own, Evidence-cited AI, Approval-required actions) and link
  to Landing, Pricing, and — where relevant — the FAQ section on those
  pages.
- **Prioritize high intent over raw volume.** A 50/mo phrase that
  matches a grower's actual workflow beats a 5,000/mo phrase whose
  SERP is B2B ERP procurement.
- **Do not chase:** Metrc, seed-to-sale ERP, cannabis compliance
  software, cannabis ERP, dispensary POS, commercial inventory
  tracking, state compliance tracking.

## C. Implementation notes

- Every post should internally link back to at least: **Landing**
  (`/welcome`), **Pricing** (`/pricing`), the relevant landing FAQ
  question (deep-link the section id where possible), and the
  Hardware Integrations page (`/hardware-integrations`) for
  hardware-cluster posts.
- The blog surface does not currently exist in the repo. This plan
  is docs-only — do not scaffold routes, tables, or sitemap entries
  for posts that have not shipped.
- Forbidden-language reminder (also used by the public copy safety
  scanner): do not draft posts around autopilot, fully automated
  grow control, AI controls your equipment, automatic device
  control, autonomous device control, hands-free grow control,
  set-and-forget automation, controls your lights, controls your
  fans, controls irrigation, controls humidifiers, or controls your
  equipment. Verdant is approval-required by design.
- Sitemap: `public/sitemap.xml` currently lists `/`, `/welcome`,
  `/pricing`, and `/hardware-integrations`. Add new URLs only after
  a real page ships at that URL.
